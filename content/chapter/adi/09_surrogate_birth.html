Despite losing her sons, Satyavati consoled widowed daughter-in-laws. She commanded Vishma: "You have to bear the torch of King Shantanu now. You know theology and are aware of the tradition of our clan. On my permission either you impregnate your sister-in-laws or take the throne yourself and marry. In any case, do not let your forefathers rot in hell".
Vishma said: "Mother, I can sacrifice everything on earth, heaven and hell. But I can not break my pledge. I have a plan to keep our bloodline going forward".
Vishma told Satyavati ancient practice of surrogate birth.
In olden days, when YamadAgnya Parashuram annihilated the entire warrior clan, the women of the warriors got pregnant with the Vedic brahmins, because Veda dictates that son begot by a surrogate sperm belongs to the married couple.
Once Saint Brihashpati approached Mamata - his pregnant sister-in-law, wife of Saint Utathya. But Mamata was not willing to sleep with Brihashpati. Brihashpati attempted to rape her and tried to enter her by force.
The unborn baby in Mamata's womb stopped his uncle's entry with his small feet. Angry BrihashPati cursed the unborn baby: "You will be blind".
Utathya's son was born blind. He was named Dirghatma.
DirghaAtma took to Bovinism - a practice to mate anywhere anytime like animals.
The other priests were enraged at Dirghatma's bovinism. They exiled him and Dirghaatma's sons floated him on a raft at their mother's command.
King Bali found the floating raft and took Dirghatma home. The king wanted Dirghatma to be the biological father of his children.
King asked his wife, Sudeshna, to sleep with Dirghatama. But Sudeshna did not like blind Dirghatama and sent her maid instead.
The maid - a low-caste, shudra woman - bore eleven children who grew up to be priests.
King Bali, however, kept insisting his wife. Finally Sudeshna herself went to Dirghatama. Dirghatama touched her skin and said: "You will bear five mighty sons: Aanga, Banga, Kalinga, Pundra and Suksha. Each will rule a region after their own name.
That is the way the Bali dynasty was originated from Dirghatma seed.

Then Vishma said: "Mother, let us employ a talented brahmin to impregnate late Bichitrobirya's wives".
