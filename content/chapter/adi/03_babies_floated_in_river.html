One day, young Shantanu was roaming in the banks of river Ganga. He noticed an exquisite woman in a golden dress. Shantanu was enchanted by her.

Shantanu approached her: "Oh my beauty, who are you? A goddess, or a mere mortal? Whoever you are, will you please marry me?".

The lady replied:"I am Ganga. I can be your wife. But on one condition. You must not resist any of my actions, be it good or evil. If you ever object, I will leave you ".

Shantanu, enchanted by the mysterious woman, agreed.

Shantanu was happy with his charming and caring new wife. They together had eight babies, all boys, all handsome as angels.

But every time, his wife threw the newborn into the river just after birth. Shantanu was irked by her strange act, but he did not resist.

Nevertheless, he could not help himself for the eighth newborn."Please do not throw the baby in the water": he pleaded to her. "Why do you reject the babies every time. Why?"

His wife smiled: "You want a son, I know. I will not throw this newborn away to the river. But my days with you have now come to an end".

Then she revealed her identity, told him about the curse of OctoBasus...
